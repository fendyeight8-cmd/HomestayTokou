Why Choose Us

A Home Away From Home

Nestled in the heart of Sapi-Nangoh, Sabah, our homestay offers more than just a place to stay it’s an invitation to slow down and truly experience rural life. Surrounded by lush landscapes and the rhythms of nature, we blend authentic local living with thoughtful comfort. Whether you’re here to unwind, explore, or connect with the community, every stay is designed to be simple, warm, and meaningful.

Main House

Our traditional heritage home, where stories are shared and friendships are made. Experience the warmth of local architecture and genuine Malaysian hospitality.

Multi-Hall

A versatile space for community gatherings, yoga sessions, or simply relaxing with a book. Designed to foster connection and shared experiences.

Palm View

Wake up to breathtaking vistas of swaying palm trees and lush tropical landscapes. A serene backdrop for your peaceful morning retreat.

Vege Garden

Explore our organic vegetable garden, where we grow fresh local produce. A true farm-to-table experience that celebrates our connection to the land.
